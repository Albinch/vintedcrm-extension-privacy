Privacy Policy - VintedCRM Extension

Last Updated: December 22, 2024

Introduction

VintedCRM Extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") is developed by Clément [Nom de famille] and is designed to work in conjunction with VintedCRM to help users manage their Vinted listings through reusable templates.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

1. Listing Template Data

When you create listing templates using our extension, we collect and store:

  • Template name (defined by you)
  • Product title
  • Product description
  • Price
  • Category, brand, size, condition, color
  • Creation and update timestamps

Purpose: To synchronize your templates between the Chrome extension and your VintedCRM account.

Storage: This data is stored on VintedCRM's servers and associated with your VintedCRM account.

2. Authentication Information

  • VintedCRM session cookies (to authenticate your requests)
  • CSRF tokens (for security)

Purpose: To authenticate you with VintedCRM's API and ensure secure communication.

Storage: Stored locally in your browser and transmitted securely to VintedCRM servers.

3. Technical Information

  • Extension version
  • Browser type
  • Error logs (only when errors occur)

Purpose: To improve extension stability and fix bugs.

Storage: Stored locally in your browser. Error logs may be transmitted to our servers for debugging purposes only.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Your Vinted login credentials
  • Your personal identity information (name, address, email)
  • Your payment information
  • Your browsing history outside of Vinted.fr
  • Your Vinted messages or communications
  • Analytics or tracking data

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  1. Provide the service: Store and retrieve your listing templates
  2. Synchronize data: Keep your templates synchronized between the extension and VintedCRM
  3. Maintain security: Authenticate your API requests
  4. Fix bugs: Debug and improve extension functionality

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your data with anyone except VintedCRM (which you have an account with)

Data Storage and Security

Where Your Data is Stored

Your listing templates are stored on VintedCRM's servers (https://vintedcrm.com).

Authentication tokens are stored locally in your Chrome browser using:

  • chrome.storage.local (encrypted by Chrome)
  • Browser cookies (with Secure and SameSite flags)

Security Measures

  • All data transmitted between the extension and VintedCRM uses HTTPS encryption
  • Authentication uses session cookies and CSRF tokens
  • No data is stored in plain text on external servers
  • Local storage is protected by Chrome's security mechanisms

Data Retention

  • Listing templates: Retained as long as your VintedCRM account is active
  • Authentication tokens: Retained until you log out of VintedCRM or clear browser data
  • Error logs: Retained for 30 days maximum

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in VintedCRM
  • Delete your templates at any time
  • Export your data from VintedCRM
  • Uninstall the extension at any time (data remains in VintedCRM)
  • Contact us with questions about your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at: vintedduplicator@gmail.com

Third-Party Services

This extension interacts with:

VintedCRM (https://vintedcrm.com)

  • Purpose: Store and synchronize your listing templates
  • Data shared: Template data, authentication tokens
  • Privacy Policy: [Link to VintedCRM Privacy Policy if available]

Vinted (https://vinted.fr)

  • Purpose: Read listing information to create templates
  • Data shared: None. The extension only reads data from Vinted pages you visit.
  • Privacy Policy: https://vinted.fr/privacy-policy

We do NOT share your data with any other third parties.

Cookies and Local Storage

The extension uses:

  • Cookies: To authenticate with VintedCRM (session cookies, CSRF tokens)
  • Local Storage: To cache authentication tokens and templates temporarily

You can clear this data at any time by:

  1. Clearing your browser cookies and cache
  2. Uninstalling the extension

Children's Privacy

This extension is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  1. Delete templates: Open the extension and delete templates individually, or uninstall the extension and delete them from your VintedCRM account
  2. Delete authentication data: Log out of VintedCRM or clear browser cookies
  3. Delete all extension data: Uninstall the extension from Chrome

Note: Uninstalling the extension does NOT delete your templates from VintedCRM. You must delete them from your VintedCRM account.
